WHAT IS
FLYING YOGA

Flying Yoga classes take influence from
traditional Yoga poses, Aerial Arts and Pilates.
D&A Flying Yoga class is a complete body workout
and stretch designed to help release the kinks
from the spine, alleviate muscle tension, tone and
elongate your muscles, mobilize and hydrate
compressed joints and extend your flexibility.

“ Flying Yoga training is suitable
for beginners and advanced practitioners. ”
